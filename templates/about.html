What is Microtasking?

Microtasking (the concept) is the idea that you have better things to do with your time than every little task which comes along. It is the process of sourcing out small, well defined tasks and having them done remotely by someone else for a resonable fee.

Microtaskingsm (the company) is a sitededicated to facilitating your experience by providing the lowest possible barrier to getting started.

How does it work?

We believe that there is beuaty in simplicity. All you have to do is provide us with a task that takes about an hour to complete, agree to pay, and we'll take care of it.
