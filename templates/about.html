What is Microtasking?

Microtasking (the concept) is the idea that your time is best spent on the creative tasks which only you can do. The small, well definable task should outsourced to a dedicated staff, allowing you to make the most of your time.

Microtaskingsm (the company) is dedicated to facilitating the above concept by providing the lowest possible barrier to start.

How does it work?

We believe that there is beauty in simplicity. Simply provide us with a task that takes about an hour to complete, pay by credit card, and we'll take care of it.

More information about how microtasking works can be found in our FAQ.
