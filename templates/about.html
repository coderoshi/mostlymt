What is Microtasking?

Microtasking (the concept) is the idea that you have better things to do with your time than every little task which comes along. It is the process of sourcing out small, well defined tasks and having them done remotely by someone else for a resonable fee.

Microtaskingsm (the company) is dedicated to facilitating the above concept by providing the lowest possible barrier to start.

How does it work?

We believe that there is beauty in simplicity. Simply provide us with a task that takes about an hour to complete, pay by credit card, and we'll take care of it.
