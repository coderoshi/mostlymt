How does it work?

We believe that there is beauty in simplicity. Provide us with a short, remote task, pay by credit card, and we'll take care of it.

Here are the general steps:

  1. Describe your microtask in as much detail as necessary. examples?

  2. Click the "Do My Work" button and proceed through the checkout steps.

  3. We'll review your request and ...

    1. If it's something we cannot do, we'll alert you via email and you will not be charged.

    2. If it's something we can do, we'll confirm our plans via email.

  4. About 1 business day following the response email, we'll begin working on your task. When we're finished, we'll charge your account and email you all results.

To see these steps in action, we encourage you to watch an introductory Video.

More information about how microtasking works can be found in our FAQ.
